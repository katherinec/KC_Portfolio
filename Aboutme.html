What I do

I am currently working as a graphic design intern at the Career Center of WashU. I work on a variety of posters and flyers that are put up around campus to promote Career Center activities. I also continue to hone my design and illustration skills through various projects. From logotypes to video game art, each project lays out new problems to solve and new ways in which to solve these problems.

How I do it

Each project begins with a prompt and an idea generation phase. Then, I being the thumbnailing process, creating smaller, less detailed designs of what the final product may look like. Depending on the project, I will take two to three of the best ideas and flesh them out until they resemble a finished product. Then, I take the best of these versions and bring it through multiple rounds of polishing until it is complete. Throughout this process, I do visual research and consult my mentors and peers on what I can do to make my work better.
